# Payment Domain Knowledge

> Cross-project domain concepts for the Payment squad. Update as you learn new entities or business rules.

## Key Entities

- **Session Credit** — Unit of value that clients use to book sessions with trainers
- **Credit Allocation** — Rules for how credits are assigned (by subscription, manual, promo)
- **Credit Consumption** — Rules for how credits are spent (per session type, cancellation policy)
- **Subscription Tier** — Plan levels that determine credit amounts and features
- **Session Type** — Categories of training sessions (1-on-1, group, etc.) with different credit costs
- **Billing Cycle** — Recurring payment periods tied to subscription management
- **Trainer-Client Relationship** — Association that gates credit usage and session booking
